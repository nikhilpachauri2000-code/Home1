About IDR

Where research meets regulated reality.

IDR is an industry‑led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and AI risk roles in regulated, high‑consequence environments such as financial services, critical infrastructure and the public sector.

Built with risk leaders who needed practitioners who can speak both regulation and code.

Our programs are co‑designed with a UK university partner and global risk leaders, blending academic insight, regulation and practical engineering to create practitioners who are fluent in frameworks and systems.

Fellows work on real‑world projects across cyber, model, AI and operational risk while mentored by industry practitioners, then deploy into client teams through secondments, embedded pods or direct hires.
